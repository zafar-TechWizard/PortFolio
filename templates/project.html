Password Management Syatem

A secure and efficient password management system built using Flask, SQLite, and bcrypt. This system allows users to securely store, generate, and manage their passwords. With encryption and local storage capabilities, your data remains safe and accessible only to you.

Empower authorities and organizations with advanced security solutions for a safer environment.
